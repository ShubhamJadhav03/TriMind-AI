# TriMind AI: Multi-Agent Collaboration System

## Overview
TriMind AI is a collaborative system featuring three specialized agents working together to produce high-quality content.

## The Three Agents

### 1. Researcher
- Gathers and analyzes information from multiple sources
- Validates data accuracy and relevance
- Provides comprehensive research summaries for content creation

### 2. Copywriter
- Transforms research findings into engaging, clear content
- Optimizes messaging for target audience
- Ensures readability and persuasive communication

### 3. Supervisor
- Oversees the entire workflow and quality assurance
- Coordinates between Researcher and Copywriter
- Validates final output against requirements and standards

## Workflow
1. Researcher collects and synthesizes information
2. Copywriter crafts content based on research
3. Supervisor reviews, approves, or requests revisions

## Benefits
- Enhanced accuracy through collaborative validation
- Improved content quality via specialized expertise
- Efficient workflow management and coordination
